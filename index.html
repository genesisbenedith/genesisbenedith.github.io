About

I build the infrastructure
of tomorrow.

From the Bronx to the cloud — I'm a software and MLOps engineer who believes the most powerful systems are the ones that run so well, no one notices them. I specialize in KPI pipelines, API services, and automation workflows that quietly hold enterprise teams together.

I hold a CS degree from the University of Arizona and a graduate background in Information Science with a machine learning specialization. Currently at TE Connectivity, I architect the data infrastructure that keeps decisions moving at scale.

But I've never been just an engineer. I'm a community builder, a storyteller, and someone who believes technology is most powerful when it reaches the right hands — especially the ones that weren't supposed to have it.
